
Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

DO’s

Maintain a professional and respectful profile, especially on platforms visible to others.

Think carefully before posting anything, as it contributes to your digital footprint.

Use polite and respectful language while interacting with others online.

Verify the authenticity of information before sharing it to avoid spreading misinformation.

Protect your personal information by adjusting privacy settings and limiting what you share publicly.



DON’Ts

Do not share sensitive personal details such as phone number, address, or passwords online.

Avoid posting offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content that may affect your reputation.

Do not spread fake news or unverified information.


Avoid engaging in cyberbullying, trolling, or negative online behavior.

Do not overshare private moments or information that could be misused by others.
